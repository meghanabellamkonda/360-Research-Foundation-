Data management & analysis

The Department of Data Management & Analysis (DDMA) deals with issues relating to the development of entrepreneurship and promotion, data management and leveraging technology for improved analytics. It is occupied in the study and analysis of major macro-economic indicators including growth, finances of the Governing body and General body, prices, external sector, Gross Capital formation, savings, final consumption expenditure etc. Time-series data on various macro-economic aggregates is compiled and carefully monitored. Regional level indicators are also compiled and analysed.
