Industries

The nature of activities in the Department of Industries cuts across the manufacturing sector. This Department takes initiatives on issues of importance cutting across different departments. The increasing interaction and engagement with the General Body, Industry associations and other stakeholders in different industry segments has added a new dimension to the scope of work.


The Department deals with subjects pertaining branches of Corporate and Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises, Public Enterprises, Industries, Chemicals and Petrochemicals.


The DI plans schemes/projects of the Development of Industries, Energy, Science and Industrial Research, Petroleum and Natural Gas etc.
